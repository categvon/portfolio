Hello, I am Cate.

I am a Master of Library and Information Science student at UCLA and the Program Coordinator at the UCLA Center for European and Russian Studies. My research interests include accessible design, assistive technology, and health information seeking behavior as it relates to disability (especially visual impairment), reproductive health and pregnancy.

I am a voting staff member on the UCLA Committee on Disability (UCOD), a Kaleidoscope Scholar (Association of Research Libraries), and the 2025 Marjorie S. Mardellis Fellow (American Library Association).
